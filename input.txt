Ben bit big but bad bob.
Bob beg Ben buy bag.
Ben buy bag but bad bug bit Bob.
Bob beg Ben bat bug.
Ben bat bug, bug bit Ben!
Ben beg Bob, “Bat bug, Bob!”
Bob bat bug but bug bob.
Bug bit bag, bag big but bug bad.
Ben bow, Bob bow.
Ben beg Bob, “Bid big bet!”
Bob bid big but Ben bet bad.
Bad bet, Bob bawl.
Ben bawl, Bob bet big but Bob bid bad.
Bad bid, Bob beg Ben.
“Big bet bad,” Ben bawl.
Bob beg Ben buy bar.
Ben buy bar, Bob buy bun.
Ben bow, Bob bow.
Bob bid bun, Ben buy big bun.
Big bun, Ben bid big!
